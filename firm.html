Our story

We can describe ourselves as IT entrepreneurs. The words which describe our organization are: group harmony, perseverance, proactivity, commitment, servant leadership, ownership and goal orientation. During our work & life experience we discovered our interest in travelling, all type of languages, IT related projects, Risk Management and Team Work Achievement.

We managed a portfolio of IT audits and IT advisory projects for different major accounting firms. In these projects we applied our knowledge of audit procedures and completed high-quality IT audits. Most of our former clients operate in the financial service industry. It is in this industry where we enjoy shedding light on IT risks which manifest, among others, during the development of innovative customer focused applications. Underlining these risks, addressing them and working out solutions gives us energy.

Assurance work performed:
  • Managed an IT audit team and a portfolio of IT-audit projects for accountancy firms
  • Executed standalone IT proces audits, system audits and IT audits for financial audit engagements
  • Performed periodic assessments of internal control systems
  • Made the certification proces for ISAE3402 engagements audit proof
Advisory & Support Activities:
  • Designed & implemented an IT Risk Control Framework for a national tax organization
  • Delivered an Information Policy
  • Delivered IT procedures
  • Designed IT-processes
  • Improved risk management and the design of internal controls
  • Supported rationalisation projects for ISAE3402 engagements

Main Clients: Vivat, ABN AMRO, SOAB, Zorgverzekersingskantoor Bonaire, Social & health Insurance Saint Martin, National Tax Organization Curacao
